Attn: Dear Reader.

Welcome.

Let me introduce myself by sharing that I am concerned about the state of our world. Possibly you are too...

Our economy is unsustainable, by nearly every meaningful measurement we can make, and many things we value within society are marginalised or exploited. Human worth; prosperity; productivity; our natural resources and biosphere; sustainability; self-sufficiency; fairness; democracy; life! All are sacrificed in a world that commodifies everything, including the very system itself.

To me this reflects a structure that is systemically imbalanced, grossly misaligned with humanity as a whole and can only lead to disaster for ourselves and our planet.

In short, the family home is burning and we need a change in thinking! I believe many of us share this opinion.

On that note, welcome to The Three Monkeys. Named after the Japanese maxim of The Three Wise Monkeys, Mizaru who sees no evil, Kikazaru who hears no evil and Iwazaru who speaks no evil, and in line with their philosophy I hope that this blog can affect that kind of change in you.

I hope you find the ideas and ideals within as interesting and thought-provoking as I do.

Sincerely,
Jordan Rancie.
