Dear Reader.

Let me introduce myself by stating that I am concerned about the state of our world. Possibly you are too...

As it stands today, our economy is unsustainable by nearly every meaningful measurement we can make, and many things we value within society are marginalised or exploited. Human worth, prosperity, productivity, our natural resources and biosphere, sustainability, self-sufficiency, fairness, democracy, life! All these things are sacrificed in a world that commodifies everything, including the very system itself.

To me this reflects a relationship that is systemically imbalanced, grossly misaligned with humanity as a whole and can only lead to greater disaster for ourselves and our planet. I believe many of us share this opinion.

In short, the family home is burning and we need a change in thinking!

On that note, welcome to my blog, The Three Monkeys. Named after the Japanese maxim of The Three Wise Monkeys, Mizaru who sees no evil, Kikazaru who hears no evil and Iwazaru who speaks no evil, and in line with their philosophy I hope that this blog can be a medium to evoke such a change in you.

I hope you find the ideas and ideals within interesting, thought-provoking and hopeful.

Sincerely,
Jordan Rancie.
